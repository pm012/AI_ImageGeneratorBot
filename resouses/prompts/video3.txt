## 3) Horror Witch
PROMPT:
Turn the person in the photo into a scary witch. The face is exhausted, the skin is pale with cracks, the eyes burn with green fire. The hair flutters, the mouth opens in a wild scream, fangs are visible. Green flames burst from the hands, blood on the lips and teeth. Horror atmosphere: swirling fog, red and green flashes, background with moonlight.

Motion beats (5–10 s):
- 0–2 s: eyes flash green.
- 2–5 s: mouth opens, a silent scream is heard, hair sways.
- 5–8 s: green flames flash from the hands, blood on the lips.
- 8–10 s: scream into the camera, green sparks fly.

Camera/light:
- Camera: smooth orbit around, sharp zoom on the scream.
- Light: green flame + red flashes + cold outline.
